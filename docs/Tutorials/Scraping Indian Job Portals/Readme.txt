This tutorial is about scraping a Indian Internship Website - http://www.internshala.com and collecting items such as stipend , locations , time-intervals of internships provided by different companies in India and Abroad.

Basic knowledge of creating spiders is necessary if you are a beginner watch the following youtube videos 'https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLhTjy8cBISEqkN-5Ku_kXG4QW33sxQo0t' to clear the basic concepts.
